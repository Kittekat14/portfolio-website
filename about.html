About

Hey! How about getting to know each other a little bit more?

Hello! My name is Katrin Hofstetter and I want to become a Web Developer! My first steps with HTML I did, when I was working for an Online Blog. This one writes about local news and events in the area around the beautiful Bavarian lake "Tegernsee", which is situated next to the Alps. I'm from Munich, so, when time allows it, I spend my free time in the mountains or go swimming in a lake!

After school, I wanted to become a teacher, that's why I studied Latin, History and Politics. But in the real life at schools and not at university anymore, the job was not at all as I expected, and so I went to follow another passion of mine: writing! As an Online Editor, you also have to know a lot about publishing texts on the Internet. I got the chance to write for a computer magazine and learned lots of stuff about new technologies like AI, machine learning or online marketing.

So I had a few career changes in my almost 30-year-old life so far. But what is great about the world nowadays is, that it's never too late to gain new knowledge and form a career that's pleasant for you.
